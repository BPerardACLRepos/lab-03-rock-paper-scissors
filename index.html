Scissors, Paper, & the Rock

Instructions

The computers will eventually destroy us all, unless we learn to beat them at their own game. That's why I've developed this, "Paper, Rock, Scissors Simulator 2021!" The exclamation point informs you that it's extreme. The rules are pretty straightforward: scissors cuts paper, rock bashes scissors, and paper just kind of sits on top of the rock (rocks hate this). That's pretty much it, you'd think we'd use guns, bombs, or magnets to defeat the computers, but that's just fake news computers put onto the internets. Train hard, you may be our final hope.
